## Executive Summary
This synthetic hospital operations dataset covers **950 encounters from 2024–2026** across Emergency Department, ICU, Surgery/OR, Radiology, Laboratory, Pharmacy, Cardiology, Orthopedics, General Medicine, and Outpatient/Ambulatory Care.

- Average length of stay: **2.12 days**
- Average bed occupancy: **73.2%**
- Average wait time: **27.8 minutes**
- Readmission rate: **12.0%**
- No-show rate: **7.4%**
- Treatment success rate: **86.7%**
- Average satisfaction score: **4.38 / 5.0**
- Average capacity strain score: **54.7 / 100**

## Recommended Actions
1. Prioritize ICU and Emergency Department capacity monitoring because these areas typically show higher occupancy, longer waits, and higher operational risk.
2. Track outpatient no-show trends by weekday, insurance type, and admission type to identify scheduling improvement opportunities.
3. Monitor readmissions by department, age group, and severity to support care transition planning.
4. Use cost burden and resource utilization together, not cost alone, to identify operational pressure points.
5. Use monthly KPI trend views to show leadership whether the hospital is improving or deteriorating over time.
